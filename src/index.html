about

I value clarity, empathy, and integrity above all. These ideals guide my approach to problem solving and life in general.

I joined Deloitte in India where I rejoice in reimagining digital product experiences and interfaces. I always look for ways of how design can contribute to a better future for humans around the world. My design approach includes a strong focus on UX as well as Visual Designs which I believe, contribute equally in delivering intuitive experiences.

Before joining Deloitte, I was part of Coditas Technologies, a UX and quality-focused digital engineering company in Pune, India. I hold a bachelors degree in Architecture Design and have contributed a fair share of my early career to designing buildings and spaces.

I have helped over 100 aspiring designers to make a switch to product design successfully and I intend to contribute to the community wherever and in whatever ways I can. My vision is to make digital space accessible to every human through simple and intuitive design.

Skills: UX Strategy, Research, Design Systems, Visual Designs, Prototyping & User Testing

My Design Philosophy

Everything is a system

When solving the problems, I look at everything as a system and try to avoid oversimplifying the problem by making sure that I dig past the surface problems to root causes.

Never Stop Iterating

I believe that iteration brings powerful results in design and I try to iterate by validating design decisions at different stages of the design process.

Ask Questions

I always ask questions and avoid ambiguity when presented with design challenges. I ensure that the requirements are very clear with the stakeholders and the team before moving on ahead.

Design for Users

If I am designing for the end-users, keeping in mind, their pain-points and their actions, then ultimately it will profit the business. After all, ‚Äúgood design is good business‚Äù.
